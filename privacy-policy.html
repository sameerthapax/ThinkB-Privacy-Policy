Thank you for using ThinkB. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and your rights related to your personal information when using our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
  • Device Information: Device OS, model, language, IP address, crash logs, and performance data for diagnostics.
  • User-Provided Information: Quiz preferences, quiz history, notification settings, uploaded PDF files (stored only on device).
  • Subscription & Purchase Data: Subscription status and entitlement information collected via RevenueCat. We do not collect or store any credit card or billing details.
  • Ad Data (Free Users): Non-personalized identifiers used for advertising, such as AdMob's Advertising ID (AAID or IDFA).
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To generate personalized quizzes based on user-provided content.
  • To manage subscription levels and unlock premium features.
  • To send scheduled push notifications and reminders, if enabled.
  • To improve performance, track bugs, and enhance user experience.
  • To display ads (free users only).
3. Data Storage and Security
  • All quiz history and study materials are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (AsyncStorage or SecuredStorageAsync).
  • We do not upload or store your documents or quizzes on any external server unless required for AI model generation (for Pro/Advanced users only).
  • We apply best practices to protect data from unauthorized access or loss.
4. Third-Party Services We Use
  • RevenueCat: Handles in-app subscriptions and entitlement checks. [Privacy Policy]
  • Google AdMob: Displays non-personalized ads to free-tier users. [Privacy Policy]
  • OpenAI (Advanced & Pro Users): AI-generated quizzes may use anonymized content for model queries. [Privacy Policy]
  • Expo & React Native Libraries: For push notifications, local file access, and other system features.
5. Your Rights and Choices
  • You can manage notification permissions directly in your device settings.
  • You can upgrade to premium to remove ads and gain additional features.
  • You can delete your app data by uninstalling the app from your device.
  • If your jurisdiction allows, you may contact us to request data deletion or access.
6. Data Retention
